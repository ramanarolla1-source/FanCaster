FanCaster is an AI-powered sports content creation platform that transforms verified live match intelligence into broadcast-ready content for sports creators, streamers, independent media, and digital publishers.

Built for the TxODDS World Cup Hackathon, FanCaster demonstrates how real-time sports intelligence can be converted into engaging commentary, match summaries, social media content, and video scripts—all from a single live event stream.

Instead of asking creators to watch every match while simultaneously producing content, FanCaster continuously interprets live sports events and generates publication-ready content in seconds.

The Problem

The demand for real-time sports content has never been higher.

Independent creators, YouTubers, podcasters, sports bloggers, and digital broadcasters compete to publish match updates within seconds of important events.

However, producing high-quality content during a live match requires simultaneously:

Following live events
Understanding match context
Writing commentary
Creating social media posts
Preparing match summaries
Producing video scripts

Most creators simply cannot perform all these tasks fast enough.

Our Solution

FanCaster transforms verified live sports intelligence into AI-generated content that is immediately usable by sports creators.

Instead of manually following every event, creators receive intelligent, publication-ready outputs including:

Live Commentary
Match Summaries
Social Media Posts
Shorts & Video Scripts
Broadcast Notes

The result is a streamlined workflow that helps creators focus on engaging their audience rather than constantly producing content from scratch.

Why FanCaster?

Unlike generic AI writing tools, FanCaster is built specifically around verified sports intelligence.

Every piece of generated content begins with trusted live match events before being transformed into creator-friendly outputs.

This creates a faster, more consistent workflow for sports media production.
